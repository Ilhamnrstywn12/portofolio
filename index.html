Digital Skill

Digital skills mean the ability to maneuver and utilize digital tools for certain things.

Web Designer

Design skills here are the ability to create an attractive website appearance, for example how to combine colors, choose fonts, determine the width of the content, and create a website logo.

Editor Video

Video editor is a profession whose task is to collect and process video material from various sources into a single unit according to the theme of the video that will be disseminated to the public.

Network Engineer

Network Engineer is the name of a profession whose job is to manage computer/telecommunications networks in an organization. A Network Engineer will be considered successful when the network he manages functions well, is stable, can be accessed/used by users, and supports organizational goals.

Content Writing

Content writing is a process for producing content and is sometimes aimed at marketing a product

Digital Marketing

Digital marketing can be interpreted as marketing or promotional activities for a brand or brand of product or service carried out through digital media. The goal of this marketing is to reach as many customers as possible in an efficient, relevant and efficient way.
